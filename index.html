Welcome to TrustHealthcare

Exposing the hidden mechanics of U.S. healthcare pricing

What We Do

Uncover Hidden Pricing

We investigate vertically integrated healthcare giants such as Cigna and Express Scripts, along with their affiliates like Quallent, InsideRx, and Evernorth, to expose how drug prices are manipulated through shell companies and opaque contracts.

Analyze FDA Labeler Data

We map live FDA NDC labeler data to their corporate parents, identifying white-labeled manufacturers and subsidiaries concealed from public view.

Build Transparency Tools

We are developing public dashboards and subscription tools that allow employers, journalists, and regulators to trace pharmaceutical pricing flows with precision and clarity.

Who We Are

Investigators & Analysts

TrustHealthcare is a mission-driven investigative platform exposing the opaque pricing structures of the U.S. pharmaceutical supply chain. Our work focuses on mapping corporate ownership, revealing conflicts of interest, and tracing how revenue is extracted by PBMs, group purchasing organizations, and their white-labeled manufacturers.
